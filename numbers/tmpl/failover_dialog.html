If your phone system is unreachable for any reason, we can redirect a caller to an alternate phone system, phone number or VoIP device.
Failover places an outbound call to connect to your alternate device. This will utilize another trunk/circuit on your account and bill accordingly, the same way it would if you made an outbound call from a phone.
